== Description ==
<h3>What is Squared?</h3>

Squared is an interactive grid system full of squares, text and awesomeness. Squared is responsive and gives you multiple options. It features a nice animating effect. You can have up to 15 squares in various colors in each section. It can be cloned and will look nice on every website.

Want it? <strong>Buy it! :)</strong>
